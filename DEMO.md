# 🎬 Delulu Detector — 2-Minute Demo Script

> Owner: **Person D**. Rehearse out loud twice before 3:30. The demo **only** runs on the
> sacred canned convos (the "Try a demo convo" buttons) — never live-paste unknown data.
> Presenter drives one laptop on the projector; a second screen shows `/booth` (the QR).

**Pre-flight (before you're on stage):**
- [ ] `OPENAI_API_KEY` set on the deployment (check a verdict returns `x-delulu-source: openai`, not `mock`).
- [ ] App open at `/` on the **cooked** demo. Second screen on `/booth`.
- [ ] Phone charged to scan the QR live if asked.
- [ ] Volume/mic on — the roast lines need to land with the room.

---

### 1 · Hook (10s)
> "Everyone's got that one chat they've reread 40 times looking for a sign.
> We built a machine that just... tells you the truth."

*(Beat for the relatability laugh.)*

### 2 · Live paste → analyze (20s)
- Click **"Try a demo convo"** (the one-sided *Jordan* chat), confirm **"You"** is selected, hit **Analyze my delulu**.
> "This is a real-feeling talking stage. Watch."

### 3 · The reveal (20s)
- Let the **Cooked-o-meter sweep** to the high score. Don't talk over the animation.
- Read the **verdict line** and one **red flag with its receipt** out loud.
> "91. 'You are the side quest, not the main story.' And look — every flag cites an
> actual quote. It doesn't make things up."

*(This is the laugh + the credibility in one move.)*

### 4 · The honesty beat (20s) — wins credibility
- Back out, load the **"Healthy convo demo"** (*Sam*), analyze.
> "But it's not just mean. Same machine, different chat —"
- Show the **low score + green flags**.
> "18. 'Actually valid.' It hypes you up when you're genuinely doing fine.
> That calibration is the whole point."

### 5 · Keep roasting — chat (15s)
- On the cooked verdict, hit **"Keep roasting 💬"**.
- Tap a quick reply: **"but they were just busy 🥺"**.
> "And it remembers the whole convo — so you can cope at it, and it'll call you out
> with the receipts."

*(Read the comeback out loud.)*

### 6 · The share (15s)
- Back to the verdict, hit **"📸 Save your verdict"** → show the downloaded card.
> "Screenshot-able by design. Built to get sent straight to the group chat."

### 7 · Sponsor close (10s)
- Flash the **`/booth` QR**.
> "Scaffolded and shipped with **Codex**, the brain runs on **OpenAI**, and nothing is
> stored — it runs and forgets. Scan it and find out if *you're* cooked." 🎤⬇️

---

## Backup plan (if the network / key dies mid-demo)
- The app auto-falls back to **calibrated mock verdicts** — the cooked and healthy demos
  still produce the right-feeling cards offline. Keep going; don't mention it unless asked.
- If a screen hangs: refresh, the demo convos reload instantly.
- Worst case, present the pre-saved verdict card PNG and the `/booth` QR.

## One-liners to have in your pocket
- "It's a lie detector for your love life."
- "No receipt, no flag — it never invents evidence."
- "Not cooked is a real outcome. We tested that on purpose."
